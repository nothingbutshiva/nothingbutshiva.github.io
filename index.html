Know Lord Shiva

The Supreme Being in Shaivism — creator, preserver, and destroyer of the universe.

✦
🔱

The Destroyer & Transformer

Shiva is Mahadeva — the Great God who destroys evil, ignorance, and the old world to make way for creation and renewal. His cosmic dance, Tandava, sustains the rhythm of the universe.

🧘

The Supreme Yogi

As Adiyogi, Shiva is the first yogi and the source of all yogic knowledge. Seated in eternal meditation on Mount Kailash, he embodies stillness, discipline, and mastery over the mind.

🌙

Symbols & Forms

The crescent moon (Chandrashekhar), the third eye (Trilochana), the serpent Vasuki, the sacred river Ganga, and the trident (Trishul) — each symbol carries deep spiritual significance.

🪔

The Five Sacred Mantras

Om Namah Shivaya — the Panchakshari mantra — is one of the most powerful prayers in Hinduism. Chanting it purifies the mind and connects the devotee to Shiva's infinite grace.

⚡

Shiva & Shakti

Shiva and Parvati represent the union of consciousness and energy — Purusha and Prakriti. Together, they embody the balance of masculine and feminine forces that sustain all creation.

📿

Devotion & Bhakti

Lord Shiva is Bholenath — the innocent one — easily pleased by sincere devotion. From Ravana to Kannappa, countless devotees have attained liberation through unwavering faith in Mahadeva.

About

✦

Nothing But Shiva is a humble offering dedicated to Lord Shiva — the supreme consciousness, the destroyer of ignorance, and the source of eternal bliss. Through these books and writings, we aim to share the timeless wisdom of Hindu scriptures, mythology, and spiritual traditions with readers worldwide.

Every book in this collection is available to read online, free of charge. Our mission is simple: to make sacred knowledge accessible to all seekers on the path of devotion and self-realization.

"The one who sees inaction in action, and action in inaction, is wise among all beings."

"Shiva is the soul of all that exists. In his dance, the universe is born, sustained, and dissolved."
