About

✦

Nothing But Shiva is a humble offering dedicated to Lord Shiva — the supreme consciousness, the destroyer of ignorance, and the source of eternal bliss. Through these books and writings, we aim to share the timeless wisdom of Hindu scriptures, mythology, and spiritual traditions with readers worldwide.

Every book in this collection is available to read online, free of charge. Our mission is simple: to make sacred knowledge accessible to all seekers on the path of devotion and self-realization.

"The one who sees inaction in action, and action in inaction, is wise among all beings."

"Shiva is the soul of all that exists. In his dance, the universe is born, sustained, and dissolved."
